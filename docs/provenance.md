# Provenance

The workbench tracks provenance at the level of sources, passages, claims, interpretations, scores, generated indexes, and publication artifacts.

## Provenance Roles

- `createdBy`: human, ai, script, or imported
- `reviewedBy`: reviewer identifier
- `generatedBy`: script or tool identifier
- `derivedFrom`: source, passage, claim, or prior artifact identifiers
- `codebookVersion`: scoring codebook version
- `definitionRefs`: definitions used for interpretation or scoring

## Publication Rule

Generated, AI-drafted, and provisional objects must remain distinguishable from human-reviewed and publication-ready objects.
