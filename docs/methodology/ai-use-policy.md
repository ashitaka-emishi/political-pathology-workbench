# AI Use Policy

AI-generated claims are candidate epistemic objects, not accepted knowledge.

## Required Distinctions

- human-authored
- AI-drafted
- generated artifact
- provisional
- human-reviewed
- publication-ready

## Rules

- Preserve provenance for AI-assisted claims.
- Link AI-assisted claims to source and passage records.
- Mark createdBy accurately.
- Do not publish AI-created claims without human review.
- Keep confidence rationales and uncertainty factors explicit.

Commit attribution for AI coding work follows `AGENT.md`.
