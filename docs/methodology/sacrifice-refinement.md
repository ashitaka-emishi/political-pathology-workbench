# Sacrifice Refinement

Sacrifice is broader than blood sacrifice. It is the costly surrender of a human good for a perceived higher good.

## Sacrifice Forms

- time
- labor
- health
- conscience
- family-life
- dignity
- speech
- agency
- economic-security
- bodily-risk
- blood-sacrifice
- death
- killing
- martyrdom
- ritual-sacrifice

## Sacrifice Health

| Value | Meaning |
|---|---|
| healthy | Bounded, proportionate, truthful, corrigible, and ordered toward life. |
| ambiguous | Mixed or uncertain effects on persons and truth. |
| pathological | Coerced, concealed, unbounded, self-mutilating, or ordered toward preserving the order at the expense of persons. |
| mixed | Distinct healthy and pathological aspects appear in the same case. |
| unknown | Current evidence is insufficient. |

## Sacrifice Boundedness

| Value | Meaning |
|---|---|
| bounded | Clear limits, proportionality, and correction are present. |
| partially-bounded | Some limits exist but are unstable or uneven. |
| unbounded | Demands can expand without effective limit. |
| unknown | Current evidence is insufficient. |

Blood sacrifice is an extreme form of sacrifice, not the whole category.
