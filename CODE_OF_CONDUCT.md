# Code of Conduct

This project studies political violence, sacrifice, ideology, and historical trauma. Contributors are expected to work with intellectual seriousness and ordinary human care.

## Expectations

- Be respectful and precise, especially when discussing victims, perpetrators, contested histories, and living communities.
- Critique claims, evidence, and methods rather than people.
- Distinguish clearly between evidence, interpretation, speculation, and publication-ready conclusions.
- Avoid harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Respect source-access rules and copyright boundaries.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, decline contributions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
